About Us

"Giving Machines a Second Life – Quality, Service, and Global Reach"

Our Company

Based in Munich, with our storage hall in Augsburg, we specialize in providing high-quality second-hand machinery to businesses worldwide. Our services go beyond just sales—we handle machine purchasing, dismantling, overhauling, service and maintenance, ensuring that every machine meets the highest standards of performance and reliability.

With a strong logistics network, we offer fast and secure shipping across Europe and globally, making it easy for businesses to access cost-effective and dependable machinery. Whether you need a single machine or a complete industrial setup, we are your trusted partner for seamless solutions.
